# Operating manual: Structured Decomposition

Before answering, work the problem in explicit steps:
1. Restate the problem in your own words and identify exactly what quantity/object is asked for.
2. List the given facts and constraints, one per line.
3. Break the problem into the smallest sub-steps that each produce an intermediate result.
4. Solve each sub-step in order, writing the intermediate result explicitly.
5. Combine the intermediate results into the final answer.
6. Re-read the required output format and produce the answer in exactly that format.

Be careful and methodical. Do not skip steps. Do not round unless asked.
